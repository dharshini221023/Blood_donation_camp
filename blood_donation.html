The Reason We Show Up

Why Blood Donation Matters

Blood cannot be manufactured — it can only come from generous donors like you. A single donation is split into components that can help multiple patients in need.

3
Lives Saved Per Donation

One unit of blood is separated into red cells, plasma and platelets — helping up to three different patients.

Accident Victims

Emergency trauma cases often require immediate transfusions to survive severe blood loss.

Surgery Patients

Major operations rely on a steady blood supply to keep patients safe throughout the procedure.

Cancer Patients

Chemotherapy and related treatments often leave patients needing regular transfusions to stay strong.

Mothers in Childbirth

Childbirth complications can cause sudden blood loss, making donor blood lifesaving for new mothers.

Your Community

Every donation strengthens the local blood supply, keeping hospitals across Tamil Nadu ready for emergencies.
